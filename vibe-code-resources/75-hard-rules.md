# 75 Half Chub Challenge Rules

## Overview
The 75 Half Chub Challenge is a mental toughness program designed to build discipline, resilience, and personal accountability. It requires completing all tasks every single day for 75 consecutive days (plus any penalty days added for failures).

## The Daily Tasks

### 1. Follow a Diet
- Choose a diet plan and stick to it
- **No cheat meals allowed**
- **No alcohol consumption**
- Must follow your chosen diet consistently for all challenge days

### 2. One 30+ Minute Workout
- Complete **one workout** each day that is **at least 30 minutes long**
- Can be indoors or outdoors (doesn't matter)
- **Walking only counts if you're wearing a weight vest**
- Must be a legitimate workout session

### 3. 10+ Minutes of Core/Mobility
- Complete **at least 10 minutes** of core work or mobility exercises
- This is separate from your main workout
- Examples: abs, planks, stretching, yoga, mobility drills

### 4. Drink 1 Gallon of Water
- Consume **1 gallon (128 ounces) of water** daily
- **Doesn't have to be plain water** - flavored water, tea, coffee, etc. count
- Track your water intake throughout the day

### 5. 30 Minutes of Intentional Self-Improvement
- Spend **30 minutes per day** on intentional self-improvement activities
- Examples include:
  - Reading (self-improvement books, articles, etc.)
  - Learning (courses, tutorials, podcasts)
  - Journaling
  - Studying
  - Meditation
  - Skill development
- Must be intentional and focused - passive consumption doesn't count

### 6. Daily Check-In
- **Check in daily** using the bot's reaction system

- This confirms you've completed all tasks for the day

### 7. Weekly Progress Photo
- Take **one progress photo each week**
- Must be in the same location and lighting for consistency
- Front-facing photo showing your full body
- Helps track long-term progress

### 8. Finances: Necessities Only
- **Spend money only on necessities**
- No unnecessary purchases or impulse buys
- Focus on essential expenses only
- Build financial discipline alongside physical discipline

## Failure and Penalties

### The 7-Day Penalty Rule
**If you fail to complete ANY task on ANY day, you add 7 days to your challenge end date.**

- The challenge doesn't reset - you continue from where you left off
- Your end date extends by 7 days for each failure
- Track your failures and adjusted end date

### Public Forgiveness Requests
**You may publicly request forgiveness in the channel to waive penalties.**

Valid reasons for forgiveness include:
- Sick kids/family emergencies
- Medical emergencies
- Legitimate unavoidable circumstances
- Other reasonable exceptions

To request forgiveness:
- Post in the channel explaining your situation
- The community can acknowledge your request
- Penalties may be waived for legitimate reasons

## Time Zone

- All days are calculated based on **MST (Mountain Standard Time)**
- A "day" runs from midnight to midnight MST
- Plan accordingly if you're in a different time zone

## Challenge Duration

- **Base Duration:** 75 consecutive days
- **Start Date:** Set when you use `/start` command (defaults to today)
- **End Date:** Automatically calculated as 75 days after start date (plus any penalty days)
- **Penalty Days:** 7 days added for each day you fail to complete all tasks

## Tips for Success

1. **Plan Ahead:** Prepare meals, schedule workouts, and set reminders
2. **Track Everything:** Use the bot's commands to log your progress
3. **Stay Accountable:** Check in daily using the reaction system
4. **Support Others:** Encourage fellow challengers in the channel
5. **Be Honest:** If you fail, acknowledge it and add the penalty days
6. **Request Help:** Don't hesitate to ask for forgiveness for legitimate emergencies

## Bot Commands

- `/start` - Begin your 75 Half Chub Challenge
- `/exercise` - Log your daily workouts (quick or detailed)
- `/weighin` - Record your daily weight
- `/summary` - View your progress summary

## Remember

This challenge is designed to build discipline, accountability, and consistency. It's about showing up every day and doing the work, even when it's hard.

**Commitment. Discipline. Consistency. Accountability.**

That's what 75 Half Chub is about.
